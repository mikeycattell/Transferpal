	<p class="content--med font24">A leading “Free-to-Play” game for Sky Games, averaging over 1.5 million players every Saturday.<br>Working closely with Sky Sports Soccer Saturday Program for promotion before kick off, you have to estimate 6 scores to win the jackpot.  New feature – you can also challenge Jeff to win a prize.</p>

<div class="grid-third">
	<h3 style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 20px; padding: 0 0 5px;"><strong>The Problem</strong></h3>
	<p>The product required a needed update to stay with Sky Sports' new branding, including visually and updating patterns. Also, needed to require more marketing pushes and showcase the new prize "Win Jeff's Million". </p>
</div>
<div class="grid-third">
	<h3 style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 20px; padding: 0 0 5px;"><strong>What I did</strong></h3>
	<ul><li>Design lead</li><li>Prototyping (html.css/marvel)</li><li>Usability testing</li><li>Faciliate Workshops</li></ul>
</div>
<div class="grid-third">
	<h3 style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 20px; padding: 0 0 5px;"><strong>Success</strong></h3>
	<p>User satisfaction rating improved, with a much higher rate of "returning" players midweek. Airtime on Soccer Saturday improved with the new looking site meaning a huge intake of new users during the program.</p>
</div>

<div class="grid-full"><h3 style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 20px; padding: 0 0 5px;"><strong>The Journey</strong></h3>	
	<p class="content--med">I discovered our user’s needs through interviews and observational research undertaken in newsrooms. I facilitated a design workshop based on these requirements, involving the full product team – it’s important that the full team contributes and has a shared sense of ownership of new features.</p>

	<p class="content--med">I sketched up the main user journeys (one of which is shown above) based on our existing design patterns, then worked with a Front End engineer to produce a mocked up javascript prototype. I took this prototype to several newsrooms for usability testing (shown below).</p>
